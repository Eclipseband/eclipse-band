We're ECLIPSE, a group of teenagers who love music and, more importantly, love Jesus. We started this band because we wanted to use the talents God has given us to share His message through music.

We play a mix of Christian worship, Christian rock, and instrumental arrangements, covering songs that have inspired us and creating performances that we hope encourage others. Whether we're playing at a church, a local event, or just recording a new cover, our goal is always the same: to glorify God and point people to Him.

We're still growing as musicians, but we're committed to giving our best in everything we do. Thanks for stopping by — we're excited to share our music with you and hope it blesses you as much as it's blessed us. Make sure to tune into our frequency!
